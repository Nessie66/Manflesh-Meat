trailcam footage showing what some believe to be Skidratt

Description

Skidratt is an unconfirmed entity theorized to have escaped during the 2005 MMC-Skunkworks incident. It is said to be a dire and disgusting attempt to fuse monogastric and multigastric DNA, namely, that of a rat and a wolf. It's common knowledge that wolves devour and devour and never shatter a single fang. As far as i'm aware.

MMC denies the existence of this entity and so we have little to go off, other than the testimony of ex-Skunkworks employees (where we get the colloquial name Skidratt.)

History/Sightings

Many don't believe in Skidratt, thinking it to be a conspiracy theory. Others passionately attempt to prove it's existence.

There have been no confirmed sightings, but there are countless examples of enthusiasts claiming to have seen the beast. Not much else is known except that it may be dangerous.
