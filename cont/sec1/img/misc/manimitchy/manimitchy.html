my heart is a plastic ball with a smaller ball inside and when you shake me I make a rattling noise,
my arms and legs are easily bendable,
but my head pops off if you pull too hard.
and then I start flailing about.
and sometimes its hard to resist screaming loud noises in peoples faces
just to get them to shut up
and to spin around and explode
then go back to whatever it was you weren't doing.
