The Defeat of the Leg Lurker and the Monogastric Manifesto


GD, a life-long resident of this town has been raised, just as you have, in fear of what lopes throughout the hills around town. The thought that the only thing keeping us safe from those lurking beats reaching down to us from upon those hills is the warm light of the town. The town would not act as our protector forever reasoned GD and so the MM (Monogastric Manifesto) was created. A philosophy born from pure flesh digested and chewed and digested again as nature intended! GD resolved to rid these hills of the beastly animals barely able to digest flesh...


The first great victory came after GD led a posse of men armed with tools fashioned from scrap metal and coaxial cable to the lair of the leg lurker. The beast reared its four ugly feet and lashed out ingjuring many, GD included. But as it slunk bat into its pit a young man distinguished himself from the rest by leaping forwards and severing the head of the leg lurker with a fibre optic flash, but as the head fell he was tragically crushed deep into the earth tumbling down the pit of the leg lurkers lair, still firmly grasping at its gigantic head, never to be seen again. That man was the legendary Coaxial Max, may he forever rest in pits.


Coaxial Max sacrificed himself and left behind the great fibre route blazing a trail for all future remedies to the single stomached disease.
