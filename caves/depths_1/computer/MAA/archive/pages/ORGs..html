History/Sightings

Derived from the MOAORG and developed in the Skunkworks, MMC made 3 of these things and for a long time was able to psionically control them with their professional (and usually unwilling) SSScs. Nothing about it was moral or right but they were infinitely valuable for their ability to digest the indigestible monogastric monsters that were previously going to landfill.

The MOAORG was discovered deep underground during the 1997 caves expedition between the dogmatic deadLink and the potential pits. They stole it away and used it to grown their own Oil Refinery Girls. The main aim was really just to stop the "gross" chewing of cud but it clearly went further than that didn't it!

Creating these things was a painstaking process that cost a lot of flesh and blood. A lot of failiures were endured and it totally wasn't worth it in the end.
